Frequently Asked Questions

How do I schedule an appointment for a patient?

To schedule an appointment, navigate to the Appointments page, click "Add New Appointment", select the patient, doctor, date, and time, then confirm. You'll receive a confirmation notification once scheduled.

How can I generate financial reports?

Go to the Reports section, select "Financial Reports", choose your date range and report type, then click "Generate Report". You can export the report in PDF, Excel, or PNG format.

What should I do if I forget my password?

Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page. Enter your registered email address and follow the instructions sent to your email to reset your password.

How do I add a new doctor to the system?

Navigate to the Doctors page, click "Add New Doctor", fill in the required information including specialization and contact details, then save. The new doctor will be added to the system immediately.

Troubleshooting

System is running slow - what can I do?

Try clearing your browser cache, close unnecessary tabs, and ensure you have a stable internet connection. If the issue persists, contact our technical support team.

I'm getting an error when generating reports

This is usually caused by selecting incompatible date ranges or filter combinations. Try adjusting your filters or contact support if the error code is "ERR-204".

Patient data is not saving correctly

Ensure all required fields are filled out properly. Check your internet connection. If the problem continues, try refreshing the page or contact our support team with the specific error message.
